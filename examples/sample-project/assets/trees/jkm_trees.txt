Model information

Nadelbaum.md3	Big evergreen
Nadelbaum2.md3	Small evergreen
Nbsnow.md3	Big evergreen covered with snow
Nbsnow2.md3	Small evergreen covered with snow
xmasgold.md3	Default xmas tree
xmasred.md3	CTF Red xmas tree
xmasblue.md3	CTF Blue xmas tree
tree1.md3	Big "normal" tree with yellow leaves
tree2.md3	Big "normal" tree with light green leaves	
tree3.md3	Big "normal" tree with yellow/red leaves
tree4.md3	Big "normal" tree with yellow/red/green leaves
tree5.md3	Big "normal" tree with pink leaves (for all your Alien worlds ;) )
tree6.md3	Big "normal" tree with dark green leaves
small1.md3	Small "normal" tree with yellow leaves
small2.md3	Small "normal" tree with light green leaves	
small3.md3	Small "normal" tree with yellow/red leaves
small4.md3	Small "normal" tree with yellow/red/green leaves
small5.md3	Small "normal" tree with pink leaves (for all your Alien worlds ;) )
small6.md3	Small "normal" tree with dark green leaves
